# Bugfix for v0.1.6

and Fluxbox do that bad thingy which is dumping core, you should apply this bugfix:
<a href="/download/patches/fluxbox-0.1.6-bugfix1.patch">bugfix1 patch (v0.1.6)</a>.
<pre>
    patch -p0 < fluxbox-0.1.6-bugfix1.patch
</pre>
